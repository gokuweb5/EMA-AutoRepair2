About E.M.A Tire & Auto

With 7 years of experience, E.M.A Tire & Auto has grown into a comprehensive automotive service center trusted by drivers throughout Winchester and the surrounding areas. Our commitment to quality service, fair pricing, and customer satisfaction has made us a leader in the automotive service industry.

What sets us apart is our team of ASE-certified technicians who bring decades of combined experience to every job. We use the latest diagnostic equipment and quality parts to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.

Our Mission

To provide honest, reliable automotive service that keeps our customers safe on the road and extends the life of their vehicles. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients through transparency, integrity, and exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted automotive service provider in the Winchester area, known for technical excellence, customer satisfaction, and community involvement. We aim to grow our business while maintaining the personalized service that has defined us from the beginning.
