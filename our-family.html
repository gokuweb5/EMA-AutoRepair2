Our Story

At E.M.A Tire & Auto, we're more than just a tire and auto repair shop. We're a family-owned business that has been serving the Winchester community since 2005. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable automotive service at fair prices.

Over the years, we've grown from a small tire shop to a full-service automotive center, but our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction has never wavered. We believe in treating every customer like family, and that philosophy has helped us build lasting relationships with our clients.

Today, our team of certified technicians continues to uphold the values that have made us a trusted name in automotive care. We're proud of our heritage and excited about our future as we continue to serve the Winchester community with integrity and excellence.

Meet Our Leadership Team

Eduardo Martínez

Owner & Founder

Eduardo founded E.M.A Tire & Auto in 2005 after 15 years of experience in the automotive industry. His vision was to create a shop where customers would receive honest service and fair pricing. Eduardo oversees all operations and ensures that every customer leaves satisfied.

Ana Rodríguez

Chief Executive Officer

Ana joined E.M.A in 2010 and became CEO in 2015. With a background in business management and a passion for automotive service, she has helped expand the company's services and improve operational efficiency. Ana is committed to maintaining the family values that define E.M.A.
