Professional Scheduled Maintenance Services

At EMA Tire & Auto, we understand that regular maintenance is the key to extending your vehicle's life and ensuring optimal performance. Our scheduled maintenance services are designed to follow your vehicle manufacturer's recommended service intervals, helping you avoid costly repairs and maintain your warranty.

Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and quality parts to keep your vehicle running smoothly. Whether you need a routine oil change, a comprehensive 30,000-mile service, or any maintenance in between, we have you covered.

Common Maintenance Intervals

Every 3,000-5,000 Miles

  • Oil and filter change
  • Tire pressure check and adjustment
  • Fluid level inspection
  • Visual inspection of belts and hoses
  • Battery check

Every 15,000-30,000 Miles

  • Air filter replacement
  • Fuel filter replacement
  • Transmission fluid check
  • Brake inspection
  • Cooling system inspection
  • Tire rotation and balance

Every 60,000-100,000 Miles

  • Timing belt replacement
  • Spark plug replacement
  • Transmission service
  • Cooling system flush
  • Power steering service
  • Suspension component inspection

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my vehicle serviced?

The frequency of service depends on your vehicle's make, model, age, and driving conditions. Most manufacturers recommend basic maintenance every 5,000-7,500 miles or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Your owner's manual provides the specific maintenance schedule for your vehicle.

What's included in a typical maintenance service?

A typical maintenance service includes oil and filter change, fluid level checks and top-offs, tire pressure check and adjustment, brake inspection, battery test, and a visual inspection of belts, hoses, and other key components. More comprehensive services at higher mileage intervals may include additional items like filter replacements and system flushes.

Can I skip scheduled maintenance if my car seems to be running fine?

We don't recommend skipping scheduled maintenance, even if your vehicle appears to be running well. Many issues develop gradually and may not be noticeable until significant damage has occurred. Regular maintenance helps catch and address these issues early, potentially saving you from expensive repairs down the road.

Will you keep a record of my maintenance history?

Yes, we maintain detailed digital records of all services performed on your vehicle. This documentation is valuable for warranty purposes, helps us track your vehicle's maintenance needs, and can increase your vehicle's resale value by demonstrating a history of proper care.

Do you use OEM or aftermarket parts for maintenance services?

We offer both OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and high-quality aftermarket parts, depending on your preference and needs. Our technicians can explain the pros and cons of each option for your specific vehicle and help you make the best choice.
