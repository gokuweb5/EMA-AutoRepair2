When to Replace Your Accessories

Wiper Blades

  • Streaking or smearing on the windshield
  • Squeaking or chattering sounds during operation
  • Visible cracks, splits, or missing pieces in the rubber
  • Blades skipping or jumping across the windshield
  • Reduced visibility during rain or snow
  • Every 6-12 months as preventative maintenance

Light Bulbs

  • When a bulb burns out completely
  • Dimming or flickering lights
  • Uneven headlight brightness
  • After a warning light appears on your dashboard
  • Discoloration or fogging inside the bulb
  • Preventatively in pairs (especially headlights)

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my wiper blades?

Most automotive experts recommend replacing your wiper blades every 6-12 months, depending on your climate and usage. If you notice streaking, skipping, or squeaking, it's time for a replacement regardless of age.

Should I replace both headlight bulbs at the same time?

Yes, it's generally recommended to replace both headlight bulbs at the same time, even if only one has burned out. Headlight bulbs tend to dim and wear out at similar rates, and replacing them in pairs ensures balanced lighting for optimal visibility and safety.

What type of wiper blades are best for my vehicle?

The best wiper blades for your vehicle depend on your local climate and driving conditions. Beam-style (frameless) wipers generally perform better in snow and ice, while conventional blades may be more cost-effective for milder climates. Our technicians can help you select the right type and size for your specific vehicle model.

Can I upgrade my halogen headlights to LED or HID?

In many cases, yes, but it depends on your vehicle's electrical system and housing design. LED and HID lights offer better visibility but may require additional components for proper installation. Our technicians can advise you on compatible upgrade options for your specific vehicle.

Do you carry accessories for all vehicle makes and models?

We stock a wide range of accessories for most common vehicle makes and models. For specialty or less common vehicles, we can typically order the correct parts and have them available within 1-2 business days. Feel free to call ahead if you're concerned about availability for your specific vehicle.
