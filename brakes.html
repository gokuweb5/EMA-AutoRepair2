Comprehensive Brake Services

Your safety is our priority with professional brake service

At E.M.A Tire & Auto, we understand that properly functioning brakes are critical to your safety on the road. Our ASE-certified technicians use the latest diagnostic equipment to identify any issues with your vehicle's braking system and provide the necessary repairs to ensure optimal performance.

We use high-quality parts that meet or exceed manufacturer specifications, and all our brake services come with a comprehensive warranty for your peace of mind.

Our Brake Services Include:

  • Brake inspections and diagnostics
  • Brake pad and shoe replacement
  • Brake rotor and drum resurfacing or replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and replacement
  • Brake line and hose inspection and replacement
  • ABS system diagnostics and repair
  • Parking brake adjustment and repair
  • Caliper and wheel cylinder service

Signs You Need Brake Service

  • Squealing, grinding, or unusual noises when braking
  • Reduced braking responsiveness
  • Vehicle pulls to one side when braking
  • Brake warning light illuminated
  • Leaking brake fluid
  • Spongy or soft brake pedal feel

Brake Service FAQs

How often should I replace my brake pads?

Most brake pads last between 30,000 and 70,000 miles, but this depends on your driving habits and vehicle type. We recommend having your brakes checked at every oil change.

What are the signs of worn brakes?

Squealing or grinding noises, longer stopping distances, a soft brake pedal, or a brake warning light are all signs your brakes may need service.

Can I drive with a brake warning light on?

It is not safe to drive with a brake warning light on. Have your brakes checked by a professional as soon as possible to avoid potential accidents or further damage.

Do you offer same-day brake service?

In most cases, we can complete brake inspections and repairs the same day. Please call ahead or schedule online for availability.
