Professional Air Conditioning & Heating Services

At E.M.A Tire & Auto, we understand how important a properly functioning climate control system is for your comfort on the road. Our certified technicians provide comprehensive air conditioning and heating services to keep your vehicle comfortable in all seasons.

Our Climate Control Service Process

1

Comprehensive Inspection

Our technicians perform a thorough inspection of your vehicle's entire AC system, including the compressor, condenser, evaporator, and all hoses and connections.

2

Refrigerant Check

We check refrigerant levels and test for leaks using specialized equipment to ensure your system is properly charged and sealed.

3

System Diagnosis

If there are issues, we diagnose the specific problem using our advanced diagnostic tools to pinpoint exactly what needs repair.

4

Repair & Recharge

We complete any necessary repairs, replace worn components, and recharge your AC system with the correct type and amount of refrigerant.

When to Get Your Climate Control System Serviced

Weak or warm air coming from vents

Unusual noises when AC is running

Water leaking inside your vehicle

AC clutch not engaging

Musty or unpleasant odors from vents

It's been more than 2 years since your last AC service

Benefits of Regular Climate Control Maintenance

Improved Cooling Performance

Regular maintenance ensures your AC system cools efficiently, even on the hottest days.

Better Fuel Efficiency

A properly functioning AC system puts less strain on your engine, improving fuel economy.

Improved Air Quality

Clean filters and components mean cleaner air inside your vehicle, reducing allergens and pollutants.

Prevent Costly Repairs

Regular maintenance can identify small issues before they become expensive problems.

Frequently Asked Questions About Air Conditioning & Heating

How often should I service my car's climate control system?

We recommend having your vehicle's climate control system inspected and serviced at least once a year, preferably before extreme weather seasons. Regular maintenance helps ensure optimal performance and can prevent more serious issues from developing.

Why is my car's AC not blowing cold air?

There are several possible reasons: low refrigerant levels due to a leak, a faulty compressor, a clogged expansion valve, or an electrical issue. Our technicians can diagnose the specific cause and recommend the appropriate repair.

How much does AC service cost?

The cost varies depending on the specific service needed. A basic AC performance check starts at $89.95, while more complex repairs like compressor replacement will cost more. We always provide a detailed estimate before beginning any work.

How long does an AC service take?

A standard AC performance check and recharge typically takes about 1-2 hours. More extensive repairs may take longer. We'll provide you with a time estimate when you schedule your service.

Is it bad to run the AC in my car all the time?

Modern vehicle AC systems are designed for regular use and running your AC regularly actually helps maintain the system by keeping components lubricated. However, having your system regularly serviced is still important for optimal performance and longevity.
