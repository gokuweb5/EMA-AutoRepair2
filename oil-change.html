Expert Oil Change Services

At E.M.A Tire & Auto, we understand that regular oil changes are one of the most important maintenance services for your vehicle. Fresh, clean oil ensures your engine runs smoothly, efficiently, and has a longer lifespan.

Our professional oil change service includes:

  • Draining old, contaminated oil
  • Replacing the oil filter
  • Adding new, high-quality oil that meets your vehicle's specifications
  • Checking and topping off other essential fluids
  • Performing a complimentary multi-point inspection

Why Regular Oil Changes Matter

Engine oil lubricates the moving parts in your engine, reducing friction and preventing wear. It also helps cool engine components, clean out debris, and improve fuel efficiency. Over time, oil breaks down and becomes contaminated, making it less effective at protecting your engine.

Schedule Your Oil Change Today

Don't wait until your engine shows signs of trouble. Regular oil changes are an affordable way to protect your investment and avoid costly repairs.

Types of Oil We Offer

We offer various types of oil to meet your vehicle's specific requirements:

Conventional Oil

Standard oil for normal driving conditions. Recommended for drivers with low-mileage, simple engines and regular driving habits.

Synthetic Blend Oil

A mix of conventional and synthetic oils, offering better protection than conventional oil at a more affordable price than full synthetic.

Full Synthetic Oil

Premium oil offering the best engine protection, especially in extreme temperatures. Ideal for high-performance engines and severe driving conditions.

High-Mileage Oil

Specially formulated for vehicles with over 75,000 miles, containing additives that help reduce oil consumption and prevent leaks in older engines.

When to Get an Oil Change

While the old rule of thumb was to change your oil every 3,000 miles, modern vehicles and oils have different requirements. Here are some indicators that it's time for an oil change:

Following Manufacturer Recommendations

Most modern vehicles recommend oil changes between 5,000 and 10,000 miles. Check your owner's manual for the specific interval for your vehicle.

Dashboard Oil Light

If your oil light comes on, it's crucial to check your oil level immediately and schedule an oil change as soon as possible.

Dark, Dirty Oil

Fresh oil is amber in color. If your oil appears dark and dirty on the dipstick, it's time for a change.

Engine Noise

If your engine is running louder than usual, it could be due to insufficient oil or oil that's lost its lubricating properties.

Frequently Asked Questions About Oil Changes

How often should I change my oil?

It depends on your vehicle, driving habits, and the type of oil used. Most modern vehicles with synthetic oil can go 5,000 to 10,000 miles between oil changes. However, if you frequently drive in severe conditions (stop-and-go traffic, extreme temperatures, towing), you may need more frequent changes. Always refer to your owner's manual for the manufacturer's recommendation.

What type of oil is best for my car?

The best oil for your vehicle is the one recommended in your owner's manual. This will specify both the viscosity (like 5W-30) and whether conventional or synthetic oil is required. Our technicians can also help you determine the right oil based on your vehicle's make, model, age, and your driving habits.

How much does an oil change cost?

Our oil change services start at $29.99 for conventional oil. Synthetic blend and full synthetic oil changes cost more due to the premium quality of the oil. The exact price depends on your vehicle's oil capacity and the type of oil required. We often run special promotions on oil change services, so check our coupons page for current deals.

What happens if I skip oil changes?

Skipping oil changes can lead to serious engine damage. Old oil becomes thick and contaminated, reducing its ability to lubricate engine parts. This increases friction, heat, and wear on vital components. How long does an oil change take?

A standard oil change at E.M.A Tire & Auto typically takes 30-45 minutes. This includes draining the old oil, replacing the filter, adding new oil, and performing our complimentary multi-point inspection. We respect your time, so we work efficiently while ensuring the job is done right.
